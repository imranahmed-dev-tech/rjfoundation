    Privacy Policy
    last update: 30 jun 2026
    What Data We Store

    Your email, name, profile picture, and the code/project files you create are stored on our servers — solely tied to your account.

    Data Security

    Your data is never sold or shared with third parties. It is kept encrypted only on (Google)'s secure servers.

    Local Storage & Cookies

    Temporary data is kept in your browser's local storage so you can keep working offline; it stays only on your device.

    Your Rights

    At any time, you can export or completely delete all your data from Settings (Clear All Data).

    Policy Changes

    Features may change as part of site updates, and this policy may also change from time to time.

    English Summary

    We store your account info (email, name, photo) and your project files via database. Data is never sold or shared with third parties. You can export or permanently delete your data anytime from Settings. This policy may change as the app evolves.
